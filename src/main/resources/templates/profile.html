Introduction

Hello again!

My full name is Henry Patrick Karugendo. I have spent most of my life in Ireland but I was not born here. That was Uganda in the East of Africa also known as the pearl of Africa! I came to Ireland in 2000 and as such you can say I am more Irish than anything really!

Interestingly I do not have an accent so don’t be surprised when we meet and there is no Dubliner accent to be heard! There is many reasons for this but the main one being that I am a quiet person by nature! I am also quite laid back! Unless I am invited by friends, I will not go out partying and clubbing! While that might mean that I am a Gamer or anti-social, that is not the case!

I rarely game, just an FPS once in a blue moon, usually COD series! No, most of my time is spent tinkering with Eclipse or IntelliJ and Java when I am not watching some movie or Japanese reality show (another reason why I have no accent, I spend a lot of time watching foreign shows).

You see I have an interest in foreign culture, like most people, Japan first of all! In fact I visited Tokyo two years ago and took some pics so visit the gallery to have a look! My trip there is what got me started in Photography too which is why I bought my very first DSLR to take with me on that trip! That was also the first time I went out of Dublin since I got here!

I consider myself friendly unless given a big reason to be on guard, dedicate a lot to my work, which is one of the reasons why I am still working at my current employer. Another reason is that, I am finding it hard to get a job relevant to my degree. I guess I was naïve to think I was set once I got one!

I have not given up yet on finding my dream job which is of course a Java Developer and eventually moving on to be an Analyst Manager! While I make my way to that dream, I will be here tinkering with Java, watching movies, getting lost in other cultures and enjoying life!
